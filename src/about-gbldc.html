About Greater Bulacan Livelihood Development Cooperative

The Greater Bulacan Livelihood Development Cooperative (GBLDC) is a community-based cooperative dedicated to empowering individuals and families in Bulacan through accessible financial services, livelihood programs, and social development initiatives. Our mission is to foster economic growth, financial inclusion, and sustainable development for our members and the wider community.

  • Inclusive financial solutions
  • Livelihood and training programs
  • Community-driven social services
Become a Member

Mission

To provide quality financial products, livelihood opportunities, and social services that uplift the lives of our members and contribute to the progress of Bulacan.

Vision

To be the leading cooperative in Bulacan, recognized for excellence in service, innovation, and community impact.

  • Service
