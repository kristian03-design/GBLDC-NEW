Learn More About Applying for a Loan

GBLDC offers a variety of loan products to meet your financial needs. Our loan application process is simple and straightforward.

Why Get a Loan from GBLDC?

We provide our members with accessible and affordable loan products to help them achieve their financial goals.

Competitive Interest Rates

Enjoy low-interest rates on all our loan products, making them more affordable for our members.

Flexible Payment Terms

We offer flexible payment terms that can be tailored to your financial situation.

Fast Approval Process

Our streamlined loan application process ensures that you get your loan approved in the shortest time possible.

Loan Application Requirements

Please prepare the following documents to apply for a loan.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Membership Status: Must be a member of GBLDC in good standing.
  • Age Requirement: Must be a 25 years old and above (legal age for financial commitments)
  • Residency: Resident of Bulacan province or nearby areas within our service coverage
  • Financial Stability: Must have stable income source (employed, business owner, or regular income)
  • Cooperative Principles: Agrees to abide by cooperative principles and values of mutual help and solidarity
  • Active Participation: Commits to active participation in cooperative activities and meetings
  • Good Standing: No derogatory records or blacklisted status from other financial institutions

Required Documents

  • Valid Government ID: Driver's License, UMID, SSS ID, Postal ID, or Passport (original and photocopy)
  • Proof of Address: Recent utility bill, barangay certificate, or lease contract (not older than 3 months)
  • Recent Photographs: 2x2 recent photographs (2 copies) with white background
  • Proof of Income: Certificate of Employment, Business Permit, or ITR for self-employed individuals
  • Initial Capital Deposit: Minimum share capital and membership fee payment receipt
  • Reference/Recommendation: Character reference from existing member or community leader (if applicable)

Initial Share Capital

Minimum Share Capital: ₱ 1,500
Membership Fee: TBA

Share capital represents your ownership stake in the cooperative and earns annual dividends based on the cooperative's performance.

How to Apply for a Loan

Follow these simple steps to apply for a loan. Our loan application process is designed to be straightforward and welcoming.

1

Submit Application

Fill out the online loan application form and upload the required documents.

2

Loan Evaluation

Our loan officers will evaluate your application and submitted documents.

3

Loan Approval

You will be notified of the status of your loan application within 3-5 business days.

4

Loan Terms and Conditions

Please read the following terms and conditions carefully before applying for a loan.

1. Eligibility

All loan applicants must be members of GBLDC in good standing for at least six (6) months and 25 years old and above (legal age for financial commitments) Applicants must have a stable source of income and a good credit history.

2. Interest Rates

Interest rates are subject to change without prior notice. The interest rate on your loan will be determined by the type of loan, the loan amount, and your credit history.

3. Loan Repayment

Loan repayments must be made on or before the due date. A penalty fee will be charged for late payments. The repayment schedule will be provided to you upon loan approval.

4. Loan Application

All loan applications are subject to approval by the GBLDC loan committee. GBLDC reserves the right to approve or reject any loan application.

5. Privacy Policy

GBLDC is committed to protecting your privacy. We will not share your personal information with third parties without your consent. Your information will be used solely for the purpose of processing your loan application.
