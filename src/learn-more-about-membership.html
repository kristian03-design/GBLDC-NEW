Learn More About Membership

Be part of the Greater Bulacan Livelihood Development Cooperative (GBLDC) and enjoy the benefits of a supportive, growth-oriented community.

Why Choose GBLDC?

Experience the benefits of cooperative membership and join a community committed to mutual growth and prosperity.

Higher Savings Returns

Earn competitive interest rates on your savings deposits and watch your money grow faster than traditional banks.

Low-Interest Loans

Access affordable loans with lower interest rates and flexible payment terms for your personal and business needs.

Annual Dividends

Receive your share of the cooperative's profits through annual dividend distributions based on your patronage.

Educational Programs

Participate in financial literacy seminars, skills training, and cooperative education programs at no cost.

Community Support

Be part of a supportive community that helps each other grow through mutual assistance and cooperation.

Democratic Participation

Have a voice in cooperative decisions and participate in elections for board members and key positions.

Membership Requirements

Simple and straightforward requirements to join our cooperative family.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Age Requirement: Must be at least 25 years old and above (legal age for financial commitments)
  • Residency: Resident of Bulacan province or nearby areas within our service coverage
  • Financial Stability: Must have stable income source (employed, business owner, or regular income)
  • Cooperative Principles: Agrees to abide by cooperative principles and values of mutual help and solidarity
  • Active Participation: Commits to active participation in cooperative activities and meetings
  • Good Standing: No derogatory records or blacklisted status from other financial institutions

Required Documents

  • Valid Government ID: Driver's License, UMID, SSS ID, Postal ID, or Passport (original and photocopy)
  • Proof of Address: Recent utility bill, barangay certificate, or lease contract (not older than 3 months)
  • Recent Photographs: 2x2 recent photographs (2 copies) with white background
  • Proof of Income: Certificate of Employment, Business Permit, or ITR for self-employed individuals
  • Initial Capital Deposit: Minimum share capital and membership fee payment receipt
  • Reference/Recommendation: Character reference from existing member or community leader (if applicable)

Initial Share Capital

Minimum Share Capital: ₱1,500
Membership Fee: TBA

Share capital represents your ownership stake in the cooperative and earns annual dividends based on the cooperative's performance.

How to Join GBLDC

Follow these simple steps to become a member of our cooperative family. Our membership process is designed to be straightforward and welcoming.

1

Prepare Documents

Gather all required documents including valid IDs, proof of address, and income verification.

2

Visit Our Office

Come to our main office during business hours to submit your application and documents.

3

Application Review

Our team will review your application and verify all submitted documents within 3-5 business days.

4

Welcome Aboard

Upon approval, receive your member ID and start enjoying all the benefits of cooperative membership.
