Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions below.

How do I apply for a loan?

Go to the Loan Dashboard and click "Apply for Loan". Fill out the required information and submit your application.

How can I check my loan status?

Navigate to "Check Loan Status" in the sidebar to view your current loan application and payment status.

How do I change my account information?

Go to "Account Settings" to update your profile details or change your password.

Who do I contact for support?

You can use the "Contact Us" link in the sidebar or email support@gblcd.com for assistance.

What should I do if I forget my password?

Click on "Forgot Password?" on the login page to reset your password via email.

How can I delete my account?

To delete your account, please contact support or visit the "Account Settings" page for more information.

Where can I find the loan terms and conditions?

The loan terms and conditions are available on the Loan Dashboard or can be requested via email.
