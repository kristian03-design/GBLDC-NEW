Social Services

GBLDC is committed to uplifting the community through various social services and outreach programs that promote well-being, education, and sustainable development.

  • Community Outreach
  • Scholarship Programs
  • Health and Wellness Initiatives
  • Disaster Relief Assistance
Join Our Programs

Our Social Service Programs

Community Outreach

Engaging with local communities through feeding programs, clean-up drives, and livelihood seminars.

Scholarship Program

Providing educational assistance and scholarships to deserving students in Bulacan.

Health & Wellness

Organizing medical missions, health seminars, and wellness activities for members and their families.

Disaster Relief

Providing immediate assistance and relief goods to communities affected by calamities.
