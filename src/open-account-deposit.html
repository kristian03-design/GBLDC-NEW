Open a Deposit Account

Fill out the form below to start your savings journey with GBLDC.

Requirements & Information

  • Applicant must be at least 18 years old (or accompanied by a guardian for minors).
  • Provide a valid government-issued ID (e.g., UMID, Driver's License, Passport).
  • Minimum initial deposit: ₱500 for Regular Savings, ₱5,000 for Time Deposit.
  • Proof of address may be required (e.g., utility bill, barangay certificate).
  • For Kiddie Savings, bring birth certificate and guardian's ID.
  • All information provided must be accurate and verifiable.

For questions, contact us at info@gbldc.com or visit your nearest GBLDC branch.
