About GBLDC Social Services

  • Community Outreach: Programs that support local communities through livelihood, education, and health initiatives.
  • Scholarship Program: Financial assistance for deserving students to pursue their education.
  • Health & Wellness: Medical missions, health seminars, and wellness support for members and their families.
  • Disaster Relief: Assistance for members and communities affected by natural calamities.

For inquiries, contact us at socialservices@gbldc.com or call (044) 123-4567.
